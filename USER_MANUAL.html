1. INTRODUCTION

Welcome to SPECTROPY

Welcome to SPECTROPY, your comprehensive Learning Management System (LMS)! This manual will guide you through all the features and functionalities of our platform, whether you're a student taking exams, a teacher creating educational content, or an administrator managing the system.

What is SPECTROPY?

SPECTROPY is a modern, intuitive learning platform designed to:

  • ✓ Create and manage a comprehensive question bank
  • ✓ Build and administer online exams
  • ✓ Track student progress and results
  • ✓ Manage courses and learning content
  • ✓ Support multiple schools and learning batches
  • ✓ Provide role-based access control

2. GETTING STARTED

System Access

To access SPECTROPY, open your web browser and navigate to your institution's LMS portal. The system is optimized for:

  • Desktop browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
  • Tablet devices (iPad, Android tablets)
  • Mobile devices with responsive design

Logging In

Step 1: Enter Your Credentials
  1. Navigate to the login page
  2. Enter your email address
  3. Enter your password
  4. Click "Login"
Important Notes:
  • Email and password are case-sensitive
  • If you forget your password, contact your system administrator
  • Always log out when finished, especially on shared devices

Understanding Your Dashboard

After logging in, you'll see your personalized dashboard with:

  • Header Section: Your name and role in top-right corner
  • Sidebar Navigation: Quick access to all main features
  • Main Content Area: Your tasks and information
  • Quick Stats: Overview of your activity

3. FOR STUDENTS

Student Dashboard Overview

Your dashboard shows:

  • Enrolled Courses: All courses you're registered for
  • Available Exams: Exams you can take now
  • Exam Results: Your exam scores and feedback
  • Progress Tracking: Your learning progress

Taking an Exam

Finding Available Exams

Step 1: Navigate to Exams
  1. Click "Exams" in the sidebar
  2. Review available exams and their details
  3. Check: title, duration, description, attempts allowed

Starting an Exam

Step 2: Click "Start Exam"
  1. Find the exam you want to take
  2. Click "Start Exam" button
  3. Confirmation dialog appears
  4. Click "Begin" to start

Exam Interface

When the exam loads, you'll see three main panels:

  • Left Panel - Question Navigator: List of all questions with status indicators (green = answered, blue = marked for review, white = not answered)
  • Center Panel - Question Display: Current question with answer options
  • Right Panel - Timer & Status: Countdown timer, progress, and auto-save indicator

How to Answer Questions

Multiple Choice (Single Answer)
  1. Read the question carefully
  2. Click on one option button
  3. Selected option highlights
  4. Click "Next" to proceed
Multiple Choice (Multiple Answers)
  1. Read the question carefully
  2. Click each correct option
  3. Selected options show checkmarks
  4. Click "Next" to proceed
True/False
  1. Click "True" or "False" button
  2. Your selection highlights
  3. Click "Next" to proceed
Numerical Answer
  1. Click in the answer field
  2. Type your numerical answer
  3. System accepts integers and decimals
  4. Click "Next" to proceed

During the Exam

  • Mark for Review: Flag a question to review later (doesn't prevent submission)
  • Auto-save: System saves every 30 seconds automatically
  • Navigation: Use buttons or click question numbers to jump around
  • Timer Warnings: Yellow (10 min left), Red (1 min left)

Submitting Your Exam

When Ready to Submit:
  1. Review your answers in the question navigator
  2. Click "Submit Exam" button
  3. Confirmation dialog appears with counts
  4. Click "Confirm Submission" to finalize
  5. Exam is graded automatically

Viewing Exam Results

To Access Your Results:
  1. Go to "Exams" menu
  2. Click "My Results" tab
  3. Click on any completed exam

Your result report includes:

  • Total score (e.g., 75/100)
  • Percentage score
  • Section breakdown if exam had multiple sections
  • Time taken on exam
  • Pass/Fail status
  • Question-by-question review with correct answers and explanations

Creating and Managing Questions

Accessing the Question Bank

Step 1: Navigate to Questions
  1. Click "Questions" in sidebar
  2. You'll see: Search & filter panel, Question list, Create button

Creating a New Question

Step 1: Click "Create Question"
  1. Navigate to Questions section
  2. Click "+ Create Question" button
  3. Question creation form opens
Step 2: Enter Question Details
  • Question Type: MCQ (single), MCQ (multiple), True/False, Numerical
  • Subject/Chapter/Topic: Categorize your question
  • Difficulty Level: Easy / Medium / Hard
  • Question Text: Write with rich formatting and LaTeX support
  • Marks: Points for correct answer
  • Negative Marks: Penalty for wrong answer (optional)

Adding Answer Options

For Multiple Choice (Single Answer): Enter options A, B, C, D and select the correct one

For Multiple Choice (Multiple): Mark multiple correct options

For True/False: Select the correct answer

For Numerical: Enter expected value and tolerance range

Submitting Questions

  • Save as Draft: Question saved but not ready for exams
  • Submit for Approval: Admin reviews for correctness and clarity
  • Status Flow: Draft → Pending Approval → Approved (ready for exams)

Creating and Managing Exams

Creating a New Exam

Step 1: Click "Create Exam"
  1. Click "+ Create Exam" button
  2. Exam creation form opens
Step 2: Enter Exam Details
  • Exam Title: Name of the exam
  • Description: What exam covers, special instructions
  • Duration: Time allowed in minutes
  • Total Marks: Maximum marks
  • Passing Score: Minimum to pass
  • Max Attempts: How many times students can attempt
  • Shuffle Questions: Randomize question order
  • Show Answers: Allow students to see answers after submission

Adding Questions to Exam

  1. Click "Add Questions" button
  2. Search or filter approved questions
  3. Click "Select" on each question to add
  4. Assign marks per question if needed
  5. Review total marks match exam total

Publishing Exam

  • Save as Draft: Continue editing
  • Publish: Visible to enrolled students, can start taking

Viewing Exam Results

To View Exam Analytics:
  1. Click on exam → "View Results"
  2. See class statistics: average, pass rate, score distribution
  3. Click student names to see individual attempts
  4. Review their answers vs correct answers

6. QUESTION BANK & EXAM ENGINE

Understanding Question Types

1. Multiple Choice - Single Answer

Description: Select THE ONE correct option

Example:
What is the capital of France?
A) London
B) Berlin
C) Paris [CORRECT]
D) Madrid

Grading: All-or-nothing (full marks or zero)

2. Multiple Choice - Multiple Answers

Description: Select ALL correct options (multiple correct answers)

Example:
Which are prime numbers?
A) 7 [CORRECT]
B) 12
C) 11 [CORRECT]
D) 15
E) 19 [CORRECT]

Grading: All-or-nothing (must select ALL correct options)

3. True/False

Description: Determine if statement is true or false

Example:
The Earth orbits the Sun. [TRUE]

Grading: All-or-nothing

4. Numerical Answer

Description: Enter numerical value with optional tolerance

Example:
If radius = 5cm, what is the area? (use π ≈ 3.14)
Answer: 78.5

Tolerance: System can accept 78.4 to 78.6 if tolerance = ±0.1
Grading: All-or-nothing (within tolerance range or not)

Building Effective Exams

Best Practices

1. Balanced Difficulty

Recommended: 20% Easy, 50% Medium, 30% Hard questions

2. Proper Timing

Allocate 1-2 minutes per question. Example: 30 questions = 60 minute exam

3. Consistent Marking

Same marks per question OR more marks for harder questions

4. Question Organization

Group related questions by topic or difficulty

5. Negative Marking Strategy

Optional: Use -1/4 of positive marks to discourage guessing

6. Quality Solutions

Always provide detailed explanations for learning

8. FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

General Questions

Q: What browsers does SPECTROPY support?
A: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge (latest versions). Mobile browsers fully supported.
Q: Can I access SPECTROPY on my phone?
A: Yes! The system is fully responsive. All features work on smartphones.
Q: How do I recover my password?
A: Contact your administrator. They can reset your password.

Questions for Students

Q: Can I take an exam multiple times?
A: Yes, if the teacher allows multiple attempts. Check exam details.
Q: What if my internet cuts during an exam?
A: Don't worry! Answers save every 30 seconds. You'll continue from last save when reconnected.
Q: Can I change my answer?
A: Yes, you can navigate freely and change any answer before submitting.
Q: What if I run out of time?
A: Exam auto-submits when timer reaches zero.
Q: Can I see answers after submitting?
A: Yes, if your teacher enables answer visibility.

Questions for Teachers

Q: Can I edit a question after submitting for approval?
A: No, submitted questions are locked. If rejected, you can edit the rejected version.
Q: How long does question approval take?
A: Typically 24 hours. Check with your administrator.
Q: Can I reorder questions in an exam?
A: Yes, in draft exams you can drag-and-drop to reorder.
Q: Can students use my questions?
A: Only approved questions you've created can be used in exams.
Q: How do I give negative marks?
A: Enter the negative mark value when creating a question. Exam auto-calculates scores.

Questions for Administrators

Q: How do I import multiple users?
A: Use the CSV import feature. Download template, fill data, upload file.
Q: Can I export exam results?
A: Yes, click "Export" on results page. Choose Excel or PDF format.
Q: How do I ensure data privacy?
A: SPECTROPY is GDPR compliant. Use role-based access and enable audit logging.

9. TROUBLESHOOTING

Login Issues

Problem: "Invalid email or password"
  • Ensure caps lock is OFF
  • Check email spelling (typos common)
  • Verify you're using correct email
  • Contact admin if you've forgotten password
Problem: "Account locked" or "Too many login attempts"
  • Wait 15 minutes before trying again (security feature)
  • Contact admin if still locked out
Problem: "Server not responding"
  • Check your internet connection
  • Refresh the page (F5)
  • Try a different browser
  • Contact admin if issue persists

Exam Taking Issues

Problem: "Exam not available"
  • Check the exam start date/time
  • Ensure you haven't exceeded max attempts
  • Verify you're enrolled in the course
  • Contact teacher or admin
Problem: Answer not saving
  • Look for "Saving..." indicator (appears every 30 seconds)
  • Try clicking "Save" manually
  • Check internet connection
  • Try refreshing exam page (you'll resume from last save)
Problem: Timer seems incorrect
  • Refresh the page (timer syncs with server)
  • Ensure system clock is accurate
  • Timer is server-based, not browser-based
  • Contact admin if truly incorrect

Question Creation Issues

Problem: LaTeX formula not rendering
  • Use correct syntax: $...$ for inline, $$...$$ for block
  • Example: $\sqrt{16}$ renders as √16
  • Common mistake: Missing $ delimiters
  • Test formulas before submitting
Problem: "Cannot submit — solution required"
  • Add solution/explanation to the question
  • Click "Add Solution" button
  • Provide detailed explanation

Performance Issues

Problem: Page loads slowly
  • Clear browser cache (Ctrl+Shift+Del)
  • Try incognito/private mode
  • Disable browser extensions
  • Check internet speed
  • Use modern browser (Chrome preferred)
Problem: Exam becomes unresponsive/freezes
  • Try refreshing the page (you'll resume from last save)
  • Close and reopen the exam
  • Try different browser
  • Check system resources (RAM, CPU)
  • Contact admin if issue persists

Contact Support

If you encounter issues not listed here:

  1. Collect Information: What were you trying to do? What error appeared? Take screenshots.
  2. Contact Your Administrator: Provide all details and error messages.
  3. Provide Steps to Reproduce: Help them understand the issue.
